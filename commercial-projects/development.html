Overview

Client Info

In early 2022, rapper "Lil Baby" put out a request for proposals to promote his upcoming tour and NFT venture. Each NFT would somehow connect to his exclusive Cee-Lo game, where fans could compete to win the grand prize: $1,000,000 and an invitation to an exclusive yacht party.

The Goal

Design multiple tiers of custom Lil Baby NFTs, and create a browser-based mobile game that integrates the NFTs with Cee-Lo tournament gameplay.
